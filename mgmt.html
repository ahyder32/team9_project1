Khaana — Management Team

Welcome! I’m Arman Hyder, the Owner of Khaana. We cook the food we crave: hand-pressed corn tortillas right off the plancha; al pastor shaved from the trompo; slow-braised barbacoa in tacos with our roasted-tomatillo, pico de gallo, and bright habanero salsas. Lunch between classes should be fast, not rushed, and dinner with friends should feel like home.

On weekends, you can find our renowned food truck carrying Khaana all across West Lafayette—often near campus and on Sagamore Parkway—serving rotating Weekend Specials like delicious birria tacos with hot consomé, breakfast chorizo burritos, and delectable elote cups. After 8pm Thu–Sat, come in to enjoy some live Mariachi that turns the dining room into a small plaza. We pair each set with Jarritos and agua fresca deals so the night ends as festive as it tastes.

About Us

  • West Lafayette roots: our entire management team are former Purdue students; we cook for the campus we love.
  • Weekend Food Truck: rotating “Truck Specials” (Taco Trio pricing, Elote & Agua Fresca combo, late-night birria) and pop-ups at Memorial Mall, Corec, and State Street.
  • Mariachi Nights (Thu–Sat after 8pm): drink deals on Jarritos, house-made horchata, and seasonal agua frescas during the first set (Thu–Sat after 8pm).
  • Fast, high-quality cooking: hand-pressed tortillas, slow-cooked meats, and fresh salsas—served at a pace that respects your day.
  • Community first: student-org spotlights, game-day hours, and frequent donations to local food programs.

We’re proudly local and proudly Mexican in spirit, flavor, and welcome.
