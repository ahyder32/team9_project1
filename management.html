Khaana — Management Team

Welcome. I’m Arman Hyder, the Owner of Khaana. We cook the food we crave: hand-pressed corn tortillas right off the plancha; al pastor shaved from the trompo with a kiss of caramelized pineapple; slow-braised barbacoa tucked into tacos with our roasted-tomatillo, smoky morita, and bright habanero salsas. Lunch between classes should be fast—never rushed—and dinner with friends should feel like home.

On weekends, our bright terracotta food truck carries Khaana across West Lafayette—often near campus and riverside spots—serving rotating Weekend Specials like birria tacos with hot consomé, breakfast chorizo burritos, and late-night elote cups. After 8pm Thu–Sat, live Mariachi turns the dining room into a small plaza; we pair the set with Jarritos and agua fresca deals so the night ends as festive as it tastes. About Us

  • West Lafayette roots: our entire management team are former Purdue students; we cook for the campus we love.
  • Weekend Food Truck: rotating “Truck Specials” (Taco Trio pricing, Elote & Agua Fresca combo, late-night birria) and pop-ups near student events.
  • Mariachi Nights (Thu–Sat after 8pm): drink deals on Jarritos, house-made horchata, and seasonal agua frescas during the first set.
  • Fast, high-quality cooking: hand-pressed tortillas, slow-cooked meats, and fresh salsas—served at a pace that respects your day.
  • Community first: student-org spotlights, game-day hours, and periodic donations to local food programs.

We’re proudly local—and proudly Mexican in spirit, flavor, and welcome.
